Hotels and accommodations in Sarria for the Camino de Santiago

Find your ideal accommodation in Sarria, a key point on the French Way

Sarria is a popular starting point for pilgrims on the Camino de Santiago, especially for those completing the last 100 km required to obtain the Compostela. At Hostal Camino De Santiago, we offer a carefully selected range of accommodations in Sarria to suit all budgets and travel styles.

How to choose your accommodation in Sarria

Consider your budget, the type of experience you prefer, and proximity to the Camino. Hotels and rural houses offer more comfort, while hostels and albergues are affordable and social options.
